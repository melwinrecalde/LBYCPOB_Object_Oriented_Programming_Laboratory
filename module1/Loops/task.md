**Loops Practice**

Write a Java program that demonstrates the use of three types of loops: for-loop, while-loop, and do-while loop.

Your program should perform the following tasks:

**Part 1: for-loop** - Print numbers from 1 to 5
- Use a for-loop to print numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
- Each number on a new line

**Part 2: while-loop** - Print even numbers from 2 to 10
- Use a while-loop to print even numbers: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10
- Each number on a new line

**Part 3: do-while loop** - Print numbers from 5 down to 1
- Use a do-while loop to print numbers: 5, 4, 3, 2, 1
- Each number on a new line

**Example Output**
```text
 FOR LOOP (1 to 5): 
1
2
3
4
5

 WHILE LOOP (Even numbers 2 to 10): 
2
4
6
8
10

 DO-WHILE LOOP (5 down to 1): 
5
4
3
2
1
```

**Requirements:**
1. Use a for-loop to print numbers 1 to 5
2. Use a while-loop to print even numbers 2 to 10
3. Use a do-while loop to print numbers 5 down to 1
4. Include section headers as shown in the example output
5. Print each number on a separate line

**Hints:**
- For-loop: `for (int i = 1; i <= 5; i++)`
- While-loop: Use a counter that increments by 2
- Do-while loop: Start at 5 and decrement until 1
- Use `IO.println()` or `System.out.println()` for printing
